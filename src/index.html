OUR SPECIALITIES

Our partners in the G2M group, a pioneer in the manufacture of prostheses, orthotics and medical equipment, allow us to offer you the best prostheses according to your needs. The production of the components and supplies necessary for the development of a prosthesis is provided by Janton, a company created in 1945 and attached to the group G2M in 2004. The assembly of the prosthesis is provided by CSM, the whole assembly process is available on its website. A group of 15 years of experience to realize your prostheses according to your needs.

When an amputation is pending, a prosthetist can begin measuring for a prosthetic hand or arm before surgery to expedite production of the prosthesis. Amputations caused by unexpected trauma will require the patient to wait until pain, bleeding, and swelling has subsided and scars have adequately healed.
